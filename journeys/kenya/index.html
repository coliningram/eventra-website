The Journey

Where the safari began.

Kenya is where the safari began, and it still sets the standard. This journey runs from Nairobi up through Amboseli, beneath the snows of Kilimanjaro, to the Maasai Mara, widely held to be Africa's greatest wildlife reserve and the stage for the Great Migration. The route stays the same across all three tiers. What changes is where you stay, from characterful classics to some of the finest lodges in East Africa.

Where You Stay

Three tiers, one route.

The route stays the same across all three tiers. Only where you stay changes.

Nairobi

Palacina Residence & Suites

A calm, family-owned boutique base in the leafy State House valley, minutes from the city yet away from its noise.

Amboseli

Elewana Tortilis Camp

A solar-powered camp in a private conservancy on Amboseli's edge, with uninterrupted views of Kilimanjaro.

Maasai Mara

Ilkeliani

An intimate twelve-tent camp in the heart of the Mara, built for privacy and personal service.
