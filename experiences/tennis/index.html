Eventra Experiences — Tennis

Every Grand Slam. The Inner Circle.

Tennis has four moments that define a year. Wimbledon in June. Roland Garros on Parisian clay. The US Open under the New York lights. Melbourne in January heat. Eventra places you at all of them — not in the crowd, but in the room where the season is decided.

The Grand Slam calendar is the most consistent luxury event programme in sport. Four tournaments. Four cities. Four entirely different experiences of the same game at its highest level. Centre Court hospitality at Wimbledon is unlike anything else in British sport — the strawberries, the quiet, the grass, the gravity of it. Roland Garros is Paris in late May, clay dust and champagne. Flushing Meadows is New York at its most electric. Melbourne is summer, heat, and the season’s first drama.

Eventra curates hospitality across the full calendar — from debenture seats and premium lounges to private boxes and hospitality suites at every major venue. We work across the ATP and WTA tours as well, placing clients at the events that matter beyond the Slams.

The Grand Slams

Four tournaments. Four occasions.

Each Grand Slam has its own character. All four deserve to be attended properly.

London — June / July

Wimbledon

The All England Club. Two weeks of grass-court tennis, debenture seating, and an atmosphere that belongs to no other tournament on earth.

Paris — May / June

Roland Garros

The French Open on the red clay of Paris. Court Philippe-Chatrier in the afternoon sun — the best tennis surface in the world, in one of the world’s great cities.

New York — August / September

US Open

Arthur Ashe Stadium under the New York lights. The loudest, most electric Grand Slam — and a city that knows how to make a sporting occasion feel like a world event.

Australian Open and WTA Finals available on request.
