Formula 1 — Austrian Grand Prix 2026

Austrian Grand Prix 2026. Champions Club — Above the Main Straight.

The Red Bull Ring sits in the Styrian mountains of Austria — a compact, fast circuit surrounded by green hills that makes it one of the most visually spectacular venues on the Formula 1 calendar. The Champions Club places you above the team garages on the Main Straight, at the heart of the action, for the full race weekend.

The Position

Above the Main Straight

Champions Club seats are positioned directly above the team garages on the Main Straight of the Red Bull Ring. This is where the cars are fastest, where the pit stops happen, and where the race is won and lost. Three days in the best seats at one of the most dramatic circuits in Formula 1.

The Weekend

Three Days. The Full Experience.

The Champions Club package covers the full race weekend — Friday practice, Saturday qualifying, Sunday race. Three days of Formula 1 at the Red Bull Ring, with VIP lounge access, terrace views, and a programme of exclusive activities that make the weekend as memorable off the circuit as on it.

The Access

Inside the Paddock

A guided paddock tour takes you behind the pit lane wall — inside the garages, past the cars, and into the world that most fans only watch on television. A big name appearance from within the Formula 1 world adds another dimension to the weekend. And an F1 Trophy photo opportunity provides the moment to take home.

The Hospitality

The Hospitality

VIP lounge access throughout the weekend. Complimentary drinks from arrival to the chequered flag. A gastronomic dinner that matches the occasion — the kind of hospitality that makes a race weekend feel like an event rather than a spectacle. Terrace access gives you the view when you want it and the comfort when you need it.

What’s Included

Everything the weekend requires.

  • Champions Club seats — above the Main Straight
  • 3-day race weekend access (Friday, Saturday, Sunday)
  • VIP lounge access
  • Complimentary drinks throughout
  • Gastronomic dinner
  • Terrace access
  • Guided paddock tour
  • Big name appearance
  • F1 Trophy photo opportunity

Secure Your Place at the Austrian Grand Prix

Secure Your Place at the Austrian Grand Prix

Champions Club capacity at the Red Bull Ring is strictly limited. The Austrian Grand Prix takes place on 26 June 2026.
