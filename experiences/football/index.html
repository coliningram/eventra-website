Football, properly arranged

Football is the only sport where the same fixture means something different in three different cities. Old Trafford on a Saturday afternoon. Anfield under European lights. The Bernabéu when Madrid host the visiting clásico. The match is the same; the weekend is not.

Eventra arranges premium football hospitality across England's Premier League, the Champions League and Europa League, the FA Cup at Wembley, and the great clubs of La Liga and Serie A. Hospitality boxes, premium lounges, and the seats that matter at every level of the European calendar.

In England, we work with most of the Premier League's major clubs through our UK football partners — Manchester United, Manchester City, Liverpool, Arsenal, Chelsea, Tottenham, plus Newcastle, Aston Villa, West Ham, and beyond. Wembley for the FA Cup, the EFL Cup, the Adobe Women's FA Cup, and England internationals. The Champions League and Europa League finals when they reach English ground.

In Europe, the Spanish and Italian top flights — Barcelona, Real Madrid, Atlético, Inter, Milan, Juventus — plus Champions League knockout fixtures at every major stage. The continental calendar's most-watched matches, accessed through partners established at each ground.

Eventra arranges the match, the hospitality, the transfers, and the trip around it. Hotels through our bespoke travel team where the weekend warrants it. Group entertaining where the trip is for a client party. The match is the reason for the trip; everything around it is what we coordinate.
