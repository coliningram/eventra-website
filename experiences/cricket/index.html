Global Footprint

The Grounds That Define the Game.

England

  • Lord’s Cricket Ground The home of cricket. A Long Room, a storied pavilion, and the sport’s oldest rivalry at its grandest address.
  • The Oval, Kennington South London’s Test ground, with a capacity for extended hospitality and some of England’s finest corporate packages.
  • Headingley, Leeds Where the 2019 Ashes miracle unfolded, and where the crowd gives English cricket its northern backbone.
  • Edgbaston, Birmingham The most intense home crowd in English cricket, paired with first-class corporate hospitality.

Australia

  • Melbourne Cricket Ground The largest cricket stadium in the world: 100,000 seats, the Boxing Day Test, and an atmosphere unlike any other.
  • Sydney Cricket Ground New Year cricket in the harbour city, with the Members’ Pavilion and the Hill among the game’s great sights.
  • The Gabba, Brisbane An historic Ashes fortress, known for partisan crowds and the intensity of an opening-Test atmosphere.

South Africa

  • Newlands, Cape Town The country’s most iconic Test venue, set against the backdrop of Table Mountain.
  • The Wanderers, Johannesburg High altitude, fast outfield, electric atmosphere.
  • SuperSport Park, Centurion The Highveld fortress, home to some of South Africa’s most memorable Test wins.
